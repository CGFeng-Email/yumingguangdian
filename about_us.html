About us

Liquor brand cooperation, we are worth your trust

Foshan Yu Ming photoelectric technology co., LTD

Guangdong Xinrui Liuming Photoelectric Co., Ltd. belongs to Beida Jade Bird’s high-tech industry. The company specializes in the research and development, production and sales of high-end ceramic high-power LED devices and modules. The products are widely used in mobile lighting, automotive, consumer electronics, stage, security, and mobile phone and other fields. The company gathers the top scientific research,management and sales talents in the field of LED packaging at home and abroad. Fully implementing international management and automated production, the luminous efficiency and reliability of the products have reached the international first-class level. Now has more than 200 invention and utility model patents, Passed the car regulation IATF16949 and ISO9001 certification
